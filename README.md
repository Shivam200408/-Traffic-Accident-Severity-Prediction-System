# -Traffic-Accident-Severity-Prediction-System
Road accidents cause massive loss of life and economic strain, yet emergency responses are often delayed by a lack of immediate, accurate information. Traditional traffic systems are reactive, failing to analyze the complex interplay of weather, road conditions, and vehicle data in real-time. Authorities struggle to quantify accident severity (Fatal vs. Minor) instantly.
